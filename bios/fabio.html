Fábio K. Mendes (Principal Investigator)

Short Bio: I was born and raised in São Paulo, the "big city" of Brazil. My childhood was fortunately uncomplicated, ordinary, and happy; I grew up eating rice and beans, playing soccer, watching cartoons, and occasionally going to the beach, in Santos, the city my grandparents lived in. Despite my very supportive parents working their whole lives as medical public servants — my mom a pediatrician and my father a public health physician — for one reason or another I never developed an interest in the medical profession. Instead, in my late teens I discerned that a career in the biological sciences was a good fit for me, in no small part thanks to two great highschool biology teachers. And the rest is... history: you can scroll the timeline if you care to see some of the highlights.
